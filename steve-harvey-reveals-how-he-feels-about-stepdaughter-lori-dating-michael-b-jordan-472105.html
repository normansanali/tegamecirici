Steve Harvey Reveals How He Feels About Stepdaughter Lori Dating Michael B. Jordan!

July 27, 2024

During a new episode of The Steve Harvey Morning Show, the host mentioned his stepdaughter, Lori Harvey‘s romance with actor Michael B. Jordan and it sounds like he approves! Check out what he had to say!

Steve Harvey really wanted his audience and the couple’s fans to know that he likes Jordan when asked about it.

That being said, he still joked that ‘I still got my eye on him,’ just like any protective father would do.

‘I mean I like I him, but like I say to all of them, ‘I might like you. I might approve of you, but I have got a thumb sized section of my heart filled with nothing but pure hatred for you.’ Just in case I need it,’ he told on the show.
Steve then confessed to believing Michael B. Jordan is indeed a ‘nice guy,’ adding that ‘But I have this section where all I gotta do is click a switch and I would hate your a**.’

He then made reference to the title the actor got not too long ago, stressing jokingly, that ‘You’re not the sexiest man in the world to me. Let’s be clear about that.’

Well, thankfully, it does not matter whether Steve finds him sexy or not.

What really matters is that his stepdaughter does and she definitely does since they’re dating!

Lori and Michael have been making headlines, their romance grabbing a lot of attention.

Not too long ago, the stars made it Instagram official as well, posting some really romantic pics on social media.

They also celebrated her birthday recently and while it was rather low-key because of the pandemic, one source told HollywoodLife that she was really glad she had him by her side.
