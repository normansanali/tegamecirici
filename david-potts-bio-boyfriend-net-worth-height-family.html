Kavos Weekender, a new show on ITV2, has one of the legends from the previous installment David Potts.

David is originally the star of Ibiza Weekender when he joined the snow back in 2015. In 2016, he was made the Head Rep. David AKA El Jefe the most popular Ibiza Weekender representative. Since the first season, he has become a fan favorite on the program, moving up the “hospitality” food chain to the lofty position of head rep.

David has been in a number of reality TV programs, including Celebs Go Dating, Celebs on the Farm, Celebrity Karaoke Club, CelebAbility, and Celebrity Ghost Trip, in addition to his work on Ibiza Weekender.

Does he have a boyfriend now? What is his net worth? His height?

David Potts On Kavos Weekender

In 2022, David is back in Kavos Weekender. David excitedly announced the news of his return by writing, “I’M BACKKKK!! Goodbye Ibiza, Hello Kavos! Huns are you ready for the new series of Kavos Weekender? New destination. New reps. Same El Jefe 👀 Tuesday 3rd January 2023 we will be gracing your screens. Get ready huns ☀️ 🌴 #kavosweekender.”

His most memorable moment of filming the new ITV2 series in summer was mostly being back with deputy rep Tash. David said: “I think it was being back with Tash, for me. Me and Tash are really good friends. We met on the strip, we were walking down the strip, we were vibing! We got to the hotel, we were dancing, we popped open a bottle of Prosecco! We were jumping in the pool. It was fun doing it with Tash again.”

Another Rep joining him this season is Jordan Davies. Jordan announced the news sharing, “WE’RE BACK AT LAST 😎🏖️ New reps, New location, New dramas. Coming to ITV2 January 3rd.”

An ITV release teasing the new direction said: “The program guarantees the ups and downs, romances and tongue-in-cheek adventures of a holiday in one of the nation’s favorite holiday hotspots.”

Additional cast members on Kavos Weekender are Tasha Karin, Ethan Aveiro, Lucy Appleton, Jaegia, Joel Frimpong, and Tyler.

David Potts Boyfriend

Despite his fame, David Potts still managed to enjoy his single life. He was previously dating Luke AKA Luke Vain from Cardiff, someone he met on Celebs Go Dating. However, their relationship ended because “maintaining a relationship with David was always going to be difficult due to the cameras,” according to RealityTitBits.

Luke told the outlet, “It was very difficult to maintain a relationship when the only time you see each other is in front of the camera. We couldn’t grow organically, because we were never allowed to speak to each other outside of the show.”

David Potts Height

David Potts stands tall at the height of 5’10”. Since Ibiza Weekender last aired, David has been on a bit of a fitness journey. In a side-by-side transformation post on Instagram, David wrote: “Three STONE DOWN HUNS!! Ahhhhh I can’t believe it! I changed my lifestyle four months ago now and I’ve never been so motivated. This new skinny me is only another seven stone away and I cannot wait! I wanna say a massive thank you so @davidnorrispt for pushing me and keeping me motivated!”

He concluded the post with, “Also wanna say thank you to one of my best friends @hayleyfanshaw for always being there for support! OMG, you huns won’t recognize me 🤪 ps the no filter life is a bit of me.”

Speaking on FUBAR Radio, the Celebrity Karaoke Club star told hosts Bobby Norris and Stephen Leng: “I’m on like this big weight loss journey at the minute. I’ve lost just under two and a half stone.”

David revealed in great detail how diligently he has been working out: “I’m literally sat in the car park like nearly throwing up for an hour, literally after every [PT] session.”

David explained to the hosts his new diet: “It’s called Xante the company I’m working with. It’s just basically a calorie-deficit diet and then I’m also doing my personal training sessions as well with my PT who’s amazing and puts me through my paces. And then I went on holiday to Greece for two weeks and then I put like four pounds back on but I’ve still lost like over two stone.”
