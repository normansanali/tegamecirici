Simon Fuller Net Worth

September 10, 2024
blog

Simon Fuller is a British entrepreneur, artist manager, film and T.V producer. He was born on 17 May 1960 in British Cyprus. Fuller has two brothers who are Kim and Mark.

He also spent a few of his childhood days in Accra, Ghana because his father was headmaster of the military school, Burma Camp.

Fuller created the UK series Pop Idol and the US series American Idol. Fuller is also the brain and executive producer behind So You Think You Can Dance and Q’Viva.

Fuller is known for managing stars like the Spice Girls, Carrie Underwood, Steven Tyler, Andy Murray, Amy Winehouse, Kelly Clackson, David and Victoria Beckham, Lewis Hamilton, Emma Bunton, Will Young, Annie Lennox amongst others.

In 2007, Fuller was named as one of the 100 most influential people in the world by Time Magazine. He has also been named as the most successful British music manager of all time by Billboard Magazine. Fuller has also been the recipient of several awards including an Emmy.

Fuller is married to Natalie Swanston, and they have three children. His net worth as of 2021 has been estimated to be about $600 million.
