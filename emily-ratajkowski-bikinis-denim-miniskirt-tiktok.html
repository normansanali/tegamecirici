Emily Ratajkowski Brings the Heat in a Bikini Top and a Rolled Down Denim Miniskirt

September 09, 2024
blog

We may be in the middle of spring, but Emily Ratajkowski is clearly ready for summer.

To promote her new batch of bikinis for her brand, Inamorata, the model lounged around New York City while wearing the new collection.

The first look of the video, which was shared to TikTok yesterday, shows Ratajkowski in a cheetah-print bikini set, featuring a ruched triangle top with knotted straps. She paired the bikini with a dark blue denim miniskirt by EB Denim, which wore unbuttoned and rolled down to reveal the swimsuit's bottoms.

The next set that the My Body author modeled was a blood-red two-piece with a dark paisley pattern printed all over the ruched bustier top and thong bikini bottoms. She later changed into a different iteration of the paisley swimsuit, a front-tie triangle top, and styled it with a pair of faded baggy EB Denim jeans that she again wore unbuttoned.

To accessorize, Ratajkowski layered with plenty of gold chain and gold pendant necklaces. She also coordinated with gold hoop earrings and stacks of gold bracelets, plus a pair of tortoise shell oval sunglasses.
