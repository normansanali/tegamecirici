Can you machine wash silk comforter?

Avoid using detergent or spot-cleaning your silk comforter or duvet because the cleaning chemicals are difficult to remove from the fibers and can cause wear. Don’t wash your silk comforter in the washing machine or place it in the dryer.

How do you wash a silk duvet cover?

Wash no higher than 30°C with a gentle, non-biological detergent that has been specially formulated for washing silk, like Tenestar. Hang your silk bed linen up to air-dry. Do not tumble-dry your silk and avoid bleach or other forms of bleaching agent because they damage the silk fibre, destroying your bed linen.

How do you wash a silk filled comforter?

Use a gentle detergent to wash it. Do not use chlorine bleach because it breaks down the bedding or fabric of bedding. Use delicate or hand wash cycle. If the comforter is very dirt, you should start the wash cycle, and then pause it for about 30 minutes to allow the comforter to soak.

Can a silk duvet go in the dryer?

If you are in a hurry, you can also toss your comforter into the dryer on low heat. However, this method is much riskier. The dryer can still tear the fabric. Additionally, the silk will harden if the comforter is left in the dryer for more than 30 minutes.

How long do silk duvets last?

With proper care, high quality silk comforters can last for 10 to 20 or even more years. Do not wash silk with strong detergents. Do not wash silk frequently.
