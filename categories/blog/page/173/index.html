From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia. The Terrain Extender (Japanese: グランドコート Ground Coat) is a type of held item introduced in Generation VII. It lengthens the duration of moves and Abilities that alter the terrain of the battlefield. In the core series games Price Effect If the Pokémon holding this item creates terrain with its moves or Abilities, that terrain lasts eight turns instead of five turns. If a terrain has its duration extended by the Terrain Extender, the turn counter on the user interface for the terrain will indicate that it lasts for 8 turns for the player whose Pokémon used the move, while it will display that it lasts for 5 turns for their opponent.
