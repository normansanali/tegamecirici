Jung Hae In Will Be Joining The Cast Of New Drama 'Snowdrop'

September 23, 2024
blog

Jung Hae In, along with several other artists, will be joining the cast of a new drama created by 'SKY Castle' creators. The new drama entitled 'Snowdrop' features the 33 years old South Korean actors and Black Pink's Kim Jisoo and Kim Hye Yoon, whose appearances were confirmed before in August 2020.

Similarly, other cast includes Jung Hae In, Jang Seung Jo, Jung Yoo Jin in the forthcoming television series.

The cast of 'Snowdrop' (©: Soompi)

The producers of 'Snowdrop' made remarks on their upcoming project. They mentioned:

"As we have gathered a team of actors who have both top class acting skills and visuals, we will greet our viewers with an especially high-quality drama."

'Snowdrop' is brought by the pack of directors and writers JTBC's hit production 'Sky Castle.' The upcoming drama will surround the love story of a student of a prestigious university, Im Soo Ho, who unexpectedly rushes to a female dorm room with a pool of blood in his body. Also, it walks on the storyline of Eun Young Cho, who hides Im Soo Ho and takes care of his wounds even during the issues of close surveillance.
