George and Amal Clooney Have Revealed the Sex of Their Twins

August 14, 2024
blog

It's a girl and a boy for parents-to-be George and Amal Clooney. The actor's mother revealed the sex of her incoming grandchildren shortly after news broke that the couple is expecting twins.

"It will be one of each! Yes, a boy and a girl. That's what I've been told," George's mother Nina Clooney, 77, told Radar Online on Feb. 10. "How marvelous! My husband and I are extremely excited."

But Clooney doesn't know what her son and daughter-in-law will name their children yet. "I haven't been told their names," she added. "That is George and Amal's department."

It will only be about four months until the pair welcomes their son and daughter into the world. Amal, who wed George in September 2014, is reportedly due in June. The news of her pregnancy was confirmed by The Talk's Julie Chen, who announced the couple was expanding their family last week. According to Chen, the Clooneys "casually mentioned" the news to her weeks ago. "This is news that everyone celebrates, how could you want to keep it a secret?" she said.
