Usually, the Film Independent Spirit Awards air the day before the Oscars. However, the awards aired on March 6 this year while the Oscar ceremony will take place on Sunday, March 27. The Spirit awards, which went virtual due to Covid-19 in 2021, highlight and celebrate movies that are, for the most part, produced and conceived outside of the Hollywood mainstream. In order to qualify, eligible films have to have budgets of less than $22.5 million.
