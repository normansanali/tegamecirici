This Is How Much Scheana Shay Is Worth

August 21, 2024
blog

First things first, let's talk about our girl Scheana Shay. Vanderpump Rules brought Scheana into fans' hearts, as she tried to cement her spot at Lisa Vanderpump's Sexy Unique Restaurant, better known as SUR. Sure enough, she found her footing and she's an old pro now. So, how much does Scheana make? According to Celebrity Net Worth, she's worth $500,000 as of 2020. A half-million is nothing to scoff at, but how does that match up with her fellow SUR servers?

Since the Vanderpump Rules cast includes plenty of people (including some recent casting shake-ups), we'll only look at a handful. Ariana Madix is worth $1 million, while her boyfriend Tom Sandoval is reportedly worth four times that amount. Jax Taylor is worth a cool $2 million, and interestingly, his ex-girlfriend (and now ex-Vanderpump star) Stassi Schroeder has earned the same amount. Oh, and Katie Maloney-Schwartz is apparently worth $500,000, just like Scheana.

Last but not least, we've got the queen bee — the namesake of it all: Lisa Vanderpump. LVP is worth a whopping $90 million and earns $500,000 per season of the Bravo series, per Celebrity Net Worth. Wait, let's rewind that real quick... Lisa makes the same amount per season as Scheana has accumulated in her whole career? When put like that, it's quite a gap... But even so, there's no doubt Scheana's career will continue to be good as gold, and with time, her net worth will surely grow.
