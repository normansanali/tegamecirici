Luke Bryan Announces His Mind Of A Country Boy Tour

September 08, 2024
blog

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess) — Country recording artist Luke Bryan announced the details of his forthcoming “Mind Of A Country Boy Tour” which kicks off at Calgary’s Scotiabank Saddledome on April 17.

Following the tour opener, Bryan is scheduled to perform at amphitheaters and arenas across the U.S. and Canada through the Spring and Summer of 2024 before wrapping the run at MIDFLORIDA Credit Union Amphitheatre in Tampa on September 14th.

Key shows on the run include the Kia Forum in Los Angeles on August 8th, August 17th at Atlanta’s Truist Park, and Byran’s August 25th concert at Chicago’s Wrigley Field, among others.

Bryan has recruited a fistful of special guests for the tour, with Tenille Arts, Chayce Beckham, George Birge, King Calaway, Dillon Carmichael, Larry Fleet, HunterGirl, Ella Langley, Tracy Lawrence, Kameron Marlowe, Chase Matthew, Meghan Patrick, Lily Rose, Josh Ross, Alana Springsteen, Zach Top, and DJ Rock, who will appear at various dates during the run.

The tour is produced by Live Nation and supports Bryan’s forthcoming album, Mind Of A Country Boy, which will launch later this year.
