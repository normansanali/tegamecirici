How to get the Giant White Stallion in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

July 06, 2024
blog

Finding the Giant White Stallion in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom can be a bit of a difficult task. This guide will walk you through the steps you need to take in order to find this elusive horse and take on the difficult mission of taming it in the game. Be prepared for a long journey. 

What is the Giant White Stallion in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Whilst the Giant White Stallion does make a feature in Zelda: Breath of the Wild, but as a Giant Black Stallion. This particular horse is the fairy-god Malanya from Breath of the Wild, but they have now taken on the appearance of a horse in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom.

This horse is difficult to tame, but it is absolutely worth it. That’s because this horse has unlimited stamina and maxed-out strength.

Although this stallion is not the fastest in the game, by the time he reaches a full gallop and you’re using the stamina correctly, this horse can cover an exceptional amount of ground.

Where to find the Giant White Stallion in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

In order to find the Giant White Stallion, you first need to rack up some Pony Points – specifically, seven Pony Points in total. Then, you can request to sleep in the special Malanya bed.

When you do this, you will be told that Malanya awaits you in the canyons of Ibara Butte at the coordinates of (0801, -3719, 0080). There’ll be a bridge you’ll need to cross.

Once you’ve crossed it, you’ll see two NPCs excitedly talking about the Horse God. After you’ve seen them, the Giant White Stallion won’t be much farther than them. 

Taming the Giant White Stallion will consume just over two stamina wheels. You can use meals and elixirs to maintain your stamina throughout this if you need to. Press L to soothe him and keep going until you’ve soothed him sufficiently. 

You can then register him at a nearby stable. Every time you rest in the Malanya bed, he will then tell you about other steeds that are nearby with excellent stats.

That’s everything you need to know about finding the Giant White Stallion in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom.
