Inside Harper Beckhams Lavish Lifestyle, Facials, Fashion and More: Pics

September 04, 2024

Harper Beckham is living large! Naturally, the 9-year-old’s great taste is thanks to her fashion designer and former Spice Girl mom, Victoria Beckham. Now that she’s getting older, Harper’s beginning to experience the perks that come with the territory when mom’s a world-famous former pop star and style icon.

It’s not a major surprise that Harper lives a lavish lifestyle, considering she gets to sit in the front row at Fashion Week and experience mom’s glam squad. All before the age of 10, she’s had skin treatments from one of the biggest celebrity facialists and a haircut from one of the industry’s mane men, who just happens to also snip the strands of British royalty! How does a young girl get so lucky?

Most recently, the youngest daughter of Victoria and David Beckham donned a floral mididress from mom’s 2020 collection. The Victoria Beckham beauty founder posted a photo series of her in the women’s dress to Instagram and captioned the pic, “Harper’s favourite print this season!”

The pretty frock was fitted to Harper’s petite shape ahead of the collection’s official launch. “My mini me and Ruta (who made her dress) at her final fitting before the #VBAW20 show x vb,” the designer added in the caption, referring to a photo of her daughter and the dress-maker.” If you like what you see, the stunning number is currently available to purchase on the label’s website for a cool $2,150.
