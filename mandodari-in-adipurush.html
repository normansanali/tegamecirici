Who Is Mandodari In Adipurush Movie?

Let us tell you that actress Sonal Chauhan has been seen playing an important role in Adipurush. Sonal Chauhan is playing the role of Mandodari In Adipurush.

Sonal Chauhan’s Role As Mandodari In Adipurush

Sonal Chauhan impresses the audience with her expression and acting without speaking. She played the role of Mandodari, Ravan’s queen.

Mandodari never supported her husband after Sita’s abduction. Although Sonal Chauhan has not got a chance to speak more dialogues in this character, the dialogues given to her are very effective.

When Ravana brings Mother Sita i.e. Kriti Sanon with him after kidnapping her, Mandodari knows that Ravana has gone on the wrong path and his destruction is near.

Sonal Chauhan has given a tremendous performance in some scenes, whether it is the conversation between Mandodari and Soorpanakha or the arrival of Mandodari wearing a white saree in front of Ravana who is going to war, she made this character mesmerizing with her acting.
