The Real Reason Hulk And Linda Hogan Got Divorced

August 28, 2024
blog

In 2016, Linda Hogan opened up about her split from Hulk Hogan, reading a letter she wrote during an episode of Inside Edition. "I was honest, I was a good wife. I never cheated on him." She went on to declare, "YOU have single-handedly ruined our 25-year marriage and our family ... my trust, my love, my future, our future family with grandchildren, holidays, weddings, our kids' lives, homes, their ability to trust, our poor animals, friends, neighbors, your career, finances, trademarks, retirement, your legacy, your reputation, your health ... even your soul ... You lost it ALL."

She continued, "You took me for granted and never appreciated me or our beautiful family ... blaming me for your horrendous mistakes. And yet you continue in life this very way, still ... Hiding behind those sunglasses, false smile, oh ... and the bandana! Until you can really apologize, to me ... and to our children ... I will never ever forgive you." 

And, unfortunately, that drama never actually stopped.
