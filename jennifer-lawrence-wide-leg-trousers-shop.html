Jennifer Lawrence's Outfit Will Make You Want to Buy Massive Wide-Leg Trousers, Too

July 17, 2024
blog

Jennifer Lawrence has perfected transitional dressing.

As summer begins to cool into fall, the No Hard Feelings star made a casual appearance in New York City, delivering a perfect example of how to dress for the changing seasons.

CO Pleated cotton-blend wide-leg trousers

CO Pleated cotton-blend wide-leg trousers

Lawrence’s outfit today exemplified her penchant for minimalist colors and silhouettes. She opted for a billowing pair of pleated wide-leg trousers by Co, which came in a deep navy blue. For the top half of the look, she coordinated with a black scoop-neck T-shirt and a bright cornflower-blue collared button-up.

The rest of the ensemble continued the minimalist theme, with the actor wearing glossy black pumps with a kitten heel from Celine, black Lu Goldie square sunglasses, and an olive-green leather tote bag by Loewe.

Gotham//Getty Images

Gotham//Getty Images

Lawrence paired her Lu Goldie shades and Loewe tote with breezy wide-leg pants on another New York City outing yesterday morning. Accompanied by her husband, gallery director Cooke Maroney, she strolled the Upper West Side in a crisp ivory vest, black slacks with a mid-rise fit and a drawstring waistline, and black sandals. Other accessories included a delicate silver chain necklace, a sculptural silver bracelet from Belperron, and her diamond engagement ring.

Gotham//Getty Images

For Harper’s Bazaar’s May 2016 cover story, Lawrence talked about how she entered the fashion world as a then-up-and-coming celebrity.

“I think I started paying attention to fashion as soon as paparazzi started happening a few years ago,” she said at the time. “I was not ready. I was like, ‘What do I wear?’ I had to start paying attention. When I turned 25 it got easier because I know, ‘This is you, this is not you.’”
