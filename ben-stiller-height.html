Ben Stiller Height: How Tall is the Zoolander Star?

Ben Stiller’s height is 5 feet 7 inches, or 170 cm.

Ben Stiller is one of the most popular and versatile actors in Hollywood, known for his roles in comedy classics like There’s Something About Mary, Meet the Parents, and Zoolander.

But how tall is the actor, director, and producer who has made us laugh for decades?

Ben Stiller’s Height in Feet and Inches

According to various sources, Ben Stiller’s height is 5 feet 7 inches, or 170 cm.

This makes him slightly shorter than the average American male, who stands at 5 feet 9 inches, or 175 cm.

Stiller has often joked about his height, especially when he has to share the screen with taller co-stars.

For example, in the movie Night at the Museum, he had to wear platform shoes to match the height of Owen Wilson, who is 5 feet 10 inches, or 178 cm.

In an interview with Conan O’Brien, Stiller revealed that he once lied about his height on his driver’s license, claiming that he was 5 feet 8 inches.

He said that he did it because he wanted to feel “normal”.

Ben Stiller’s Height Compared to Other Celebrities

Ben Stiller may not be the tallest actor in Hollywood, but he is not the shortest either.

Here are some celebrities who are taller or shorter than him:

  • Tom Cruise: The Mission Impossible star is famously shorter than Stiller, standing at 5 feet 6 inches, or 168 cm. The two have parodied their height difference in several sketches, such as the one where they played stunt doubles for each other.
  • Jennifer Aniston: The Friends actress and Stiller’s co-star in Along Came Polly is slightly taller than him, measuring 5 feet 8 inches, or 173 cm. However, she often wore flats or low heels when they filmed together to avoid towering over him
  • Robert De Niro: The legendary actor and Stiller’s on-screen father-in-law in the Meet the Parents trilogy is exactly the same height as him, at 5 feet 7 inches, or 170 cm. However, De Niro sometimes wore lifts to appear taller in some scene.
  • Cameron Diaz: The There’s Something About Mary actress and Stiller’s love interest in the film is much taller than him, standing at 5 feet 9 inches, or 175 cm. She also wore heels in some scenes, making the height difference even more noticeable.

Who is Ben Stiller?

Although Ben Stiller is often viewed as shorter by Hollywood’s norms, his height of 5’7″ is actually quite typical for a man.

This places him in the same height bracket as several other well-known male stars, such as Mark Wahlberg, Daniel Radcliffe, and Robert Downey Jr.

Ben Stiller’s Net Worth

Ben Stiller is a multi-talented American artist, known for his roles in acting, comedy, writing, directing, and producing, amassing a substantial net worth of $200 million.

He’s most recognized for his outstanding contributions to the film industry, notably starring in hit movies like “Zoolander,” “There’s Something About Mary,” “Meet The Parents,” and “Tropic Thunder.”

In fact, Ben Stiller’s cinematic endeavors have raked in an impressive total of over $6 billion in worldwide box office earnings.

Ben Stiller’s journey into the entertainment world was no small feat.

As the son of acclaimed actors Jerry Stiller and Anne Meara, he was born into a family of established performers.

However, he was determined to make his own mark.

He initially enrolled at UCLA Film School but chose to leave during his freshman year to return to New York, where he focused on auditions and securing representation.

While his acting career initially took center stage, he soon pivoted to crafting short films.

One of these shorts, “The Hustler of Money,” garnered attention from the “Saturday Night Live” team, leading to an invitation to contribute to the show.

Despite this promising start, his tenure at “SNL” was short-lived, and he returned to independently producing his short films.

Subsequently, MTV came calling, offering him the opportunity to create his own show, “The Ben Stiller Show,” centered around his comedic short films.

Although it had a brief initial run, the show was later picked up by the Fox Network in the early ’90s, earning an Emmy for “Outstanding Writing” during its additional season before eventually being canceled.

Ben Stiller Personal Life

Ben has been romantically involved with various celebrities, including Jeanne Tripplehorn, Amanda Peet, and Calista Flockhart.

Nevertheless, he took the plunge into marriage with Christine Taylor, an American actress, in a picturesque oceanfront wedding held in Hawaii in May 2000.

The two first crossed paths while collaborating on an unreleased Fox television project.

Their professional collaboration extended to appearing together in several projects such as Arrested Development, Tropic Thunder, and Zoolander.

Ben Stiller and Christine Taylor are proud parents to two children: a son named Quinlin Dempsey and a daughter named Ella Olivia.

Quinlin even had the opportunity to follow in his father’s footsteps by voicing the character of Alex as a young chick in the film “Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa,” alongside another child actor named Declan Swift.

Unfortunately, after a 17-year marriage, Ben Stiller and Christine Taylor made the difficult decision to divorce in May 2017.

Despite his remarkable success in the entertainment industry, Ben Stiller has faced personal challenges throughout his life, including the divorce of his parents during his childhood.

Like many public figures who have experienced adversity, he has been candid about his struggles with mental health.
