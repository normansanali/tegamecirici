Golden State Warriors guard Jordan Poole recently landed a huge contract, bringing more attention than ever to him and his personal life.

Poole is one of the most talented young players in the league. He's already won a championship and many fans expect him to turn into a superstar at some point.

He's been linked with Kim Cruz, a popular blogger and artist from the Philippines. Born in 1997, the social media star has gained a huge following on her public pages. As of December 2022, Cruz has 426,000 followers on her Instagram profile.
