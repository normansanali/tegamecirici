Jessie Ware Cancels Milwaukee Show

July 16, 2024
blog

MILWAUKEE (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — British singer-songwriter Jessie Ware has canceled her November 9th show at Turner Hall Ballroom in Milwaukee, stating that the show had been relocated to the House of Blues in Chicago.

When announcing that the Milwaukee show was canceled, she reported that fans would be offered a refund or a replacement ticket for the House of Blues show for the same night.

Ware, best known for her 2012 single "Wildest Moments", Ware has been on the road hitting festivals like Glastonbury and supporting her debut studio album "Devotion." – Staff Writers
