Khary Payton Height: How tall is Khary Payton?

August 23, 2024
blog

Khary Payton stands at a height of 5 feet 10 inches (1.78 m) tall.

The American actor and voice actor Payton is known for his roles as King Ezekiel on the horror drama series The Walking Dead and Dr. Terrell Jackson on the soap opera General Hospital.

Born in Augusta, Georgia, he started acting at age 14. He was the winner of Showtime’s first annual Kid Talent Ques.

Payton commenced his career with a small role on the series Street Sharks in 1994 and had his best film role Andrew in the 2003 romantic comedy Latter Days.

As a voice actor, Payton has done many voices for films and tv shows such as the voice of the villain Ten from the Royal Flush Gang, the voice of Drebin in the game Metal Gear Solid 4: Guns of the Patriots, Ripcord in G.I. Joe: Renegades, Blade in Marvel: Ultimate Alliance, Grimlock in Transformers: Robots in Disguise and Killer Croc in Batman: Arkham Underworld.
