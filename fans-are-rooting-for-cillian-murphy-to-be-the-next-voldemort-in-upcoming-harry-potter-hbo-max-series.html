Fans Are Rooting for Cillian Murphy to Be the Next Voldemort in Upcoming Harry Potter HBO Max Series

September 09, 2024
blog

The Wizarding World is Abuzz: Who Will Be the Next Dark Lord?

The cauldron is bubbling and the owls are flying with news that has every Potterhead’s wand twitching: a new Harry Potter series is in the works for HBO Max. And while the ink is still wet on the parchment, fans are already conjuring up their dream cast. Topping the list for the role of the infamous Lord Voldemort? None other than Irish actor Cillian Murphy.

“So far, officially, there is no one from the casting confirmed, because so far, only the production has been confirmed,” says a source close to the project.

But that hasn’t stopped the fandom from diving into heated debates and speculative discussions, especially on platforms like Reddit.

Why Cillian Murphy is the Fan-Favorite for Voldemort

Murphy, known for his versatile roles in films like “28 Days Later” and “The Dark Knight,” and more recently “Oppenheimer,” has a certain dark allure that fans believe would make him the perfect Voldemort.

“The proposal of Cillian Murphy as the dark lord is more from the fans, but it could reach the ears of those who produce the series and take the suggestion,” notes a hopeful fan.

The series, which has received the blessing of J.K. Rowling, promises to stick closely to the books, potentially offering details that the original films skipped over. This faithfulness to the source material is one of the reasons fans are rallying behind Murphy, who they feel could bring a nuanced depth to the character of Tom Riddle, the man who would become Voldemort.

Other Contenders in the Magical Ring

While Murphy seems to be the fan-favorite, he’s not the only name being tossed into the Sorting Hat. English actors Christian Bale and Benedict Cumberbatch have also been mentioned. However, Murphy seems to be the most convincing choice for the majority of Harry Potter aficionados.

“Especially because they closely resemble the character of Tom Riddle, before becoming Lord Voldemort,” says a fan, making a case for Murphy.

A Long Road to the Dark Arts

With the series speculated to premiere between 2025 and 2026, there’s still plenty of time for casting decisions to be made. But one thing is clear: fans are eager to see their visions come to life.

“Let’s keep our fingers crossed!” exclaims a fan, summing up the collective sentiment.

So, will Cillian Murphy trade in his Peaky Blinders cap for a dark wizard’s robe? Only time will tell, but the prospect has certainly cast a spell on the Harry Potter community.
