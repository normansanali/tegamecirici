Eva Rodriguez Military

Eva Rodriguez is a remarkable woman with a distinguished military career as a combat helicopter pilot behind her.  Being one of the first women to pass the stringent test for pilot training in 1987 (after serving as a combat nurse), she was soon accepted and enjoyed a wonderful camaraderie with her male colleagues.

The career had huge financial rewards which meant she could go shopping anywhere in the world and own expensive cars and homes in Germany and Egypt. “I literally lived the American dream,” recalls Eva with a smile.

Born in Detroit her parents were poor and being able to advance through the ranks starting out as a combat nurse – was part of that American dream.

The downside of her military career was that she was often living on the edge, transporting patients and medical supplies in the war zones of Iraq, Saudi Arabia, and Kuwait during the Gulf War.
