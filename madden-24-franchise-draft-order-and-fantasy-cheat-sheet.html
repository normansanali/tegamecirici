Madden 24 Fantasy Draft Cheat Sheet

Madden 24 Fantasy Draft Order starts with the best quarterback from the list. With a 99 rating, Patrick Mahomes is the best in this Madden 24 video game.

To start a fantasy draft in Madden 24 you have to follow the following guideline:

  • Select franchise mood from the main menu
  • Then create a new league 
  • Choose the real-life players and current active players
  • Now, You have to choose the team as per your choice as it wonâ€™t affect your draft
  •  Go to starting point and select Fantasy Draft
  • If you want to change your role, click on change role below the starting point. You can be a player, owner, or manager
  • Now, click on starting
  • On the franchise mode home screen, choose to start your Fantasy Draft.
  • Then press the Start Drafting option.

When you are choosing the player, you have to choose a player before the time is up. The drafting process run until your team has 53 players. But, if you want more time to think you can pause the drafting process for a while.

On the other hand, if you want to finish the draft faster you can let the game do it for you after you get the players you want in Madden 24.

To get valuable players in Fantasy Draft in Madden 24 you should look for players who are not famous. But have higher ratings in some important skills like speed and strength. 

These players will be available in the later rounds depending on players and their position. You can see the draft board when the other teams pick their players.
