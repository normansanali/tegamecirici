Clint Bowyer’s Net Worth and Career Earnings

Clint Bowyer is a successful American NASCAR driver with a net worth of $40 million. He began his racing career at an early age and has won over 200 races and several championships. Bowyer’s impressive achievements caught the attention of car owner Richard Childress, who offered him a place on his team. After eight years with Richard Childress, Bowyer signed a three-year contract with Michael Waltrip in 2012.

Between June 2018 and June 2019, Clint Bowyer earned $7 million. Most of his earnings, roughly $6.6 million, came from salaries and winnings. He was the 10th highest-earning NASCAR driver in the world.

In addition to racing, Bowyer started his own racing team, Clint Bowyer Racing, in 2008. He also made a memorable appearance on the A&E television show, Duck Dynasty, and featured a camouflage-decorated limousine in a race on the show.
