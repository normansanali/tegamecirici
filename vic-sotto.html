Who is Vic Sotto:

Vic Sotto is a famous Game Show Host. He was born on April 28, 1954 and his birthplace is Manila, Philippines. Vic is also well known as, Filipino television personality, game show host, and title winner of Philippine Box Office King who has hosted the noon-time variety show Eat Bulaga!

Vic is originated from Philippines. He hosted of the Filipino version of Who Wants To Be A Millionaire. Regis Philbin hosted the U.S. version.
