WWE Selling $5,000 Official TV Authentic Title

July 13, 2024
blog

WWE has jumped head-first into the high-end memorabilia market. Their primary fan base is over 50 years old and they have some disposable income to spend.

The official WWE Shop is now selling an authentic WWE Championship Title belt for $4,999.99. This is the same quality that they use on television.

Our Official TV Authentic title belts feature 383 princess cut hand-set Cubic Zirconia, totaling 635 carats. Each stone is carefully placed onto high-quality aluminum plates with a 14k gold plating.   Weighing in at 7.5 lbs, you’ll feel like a true Champion with this amazing piece of craftsmanship. 

WWE selling really expensive replica titles is nothing new. The Fiend’s WWE Universal Title cost way more than five grand. It was also a limited edition.

This title belt might not be there by Christmas, but it might be a very good way to blow through some money after the Holidays.
