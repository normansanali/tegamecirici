Kristen Stewart and St. Vincent Make Their First Red Carpet Appearance Together

Kristen Stewart and St. Vincent made their first official public appearance as a couple when they attended a CFDA event together last night. The duo exuded an edgy kind of cool with their matching shades and tailored ensembles. Stewart, a Chanel muse, paired a blazer with houndstooth trousers and patent leather loafers while St. Vincent, better knowns as Annie Clark, went for a cropped tweed jacket, wide-leg pants and a choker.
